about

AJAD FM, an Emirati Total Facility Management established in 2002 based in UAE. We have been utilizing our international partnership and exposure to provide high quality services for over a decade.

We pride ourselves with our highly skilled and well trained employees consistently delivering excellence in the FM industry. AJAD will deliver a new experience in FM industry with value to our clients and quality service.

Quality

Quality is ingrained in our DNA. AJAD believes that providing quality services is every client’s right. It is a result of implementing excellent managerial, operational and administrative processes based on our Quality Management System in line with the requirements of ISO 9001:2015 standard.

Our aim is to provide an effective and timely service which exceeds our client’s expectations.

Health, Safety and Environment

We are committed to providing safe and healthy work environments for all our employees, clients and public at large.

AJAD believes that all accidents, hazards, injuries, and environmentally harmful actions are preventable under effective HSE Management system which is in line with ISO 14001:2015 and OHSAS 18001:2007 standard.
